Stable diffusion challenge report
What is this challenge about?
In this challenge, we used a stable diffusion model, specifically the Dream Booth stable diffusion model, to generate pictures of different shields using a prompt.
For the training, we used a database of 10 shields from the Unity Asset Store.

Below are a few examples of the generated images using the ‘Euler a’ method.
We used a keyword ‘loebda’ here instead of shields.
“A pale red loebla with a golden icon of a sword and a silver rim.”
“A bright pink loebla with a light pink icon of a lizard with a silver rim around”
“A green loebla with a light green icon of a sword and a grey rim around it.”
“A green loebda with a light green icon of a deer”
